The Road Traveled and The Road To Come

I have lived an eventful life that has resulted in many miles traveled to various parts of the world. In 2000 I joined the U.S. Army where I was trained in the aviation field and also as a paratrooper. After multiple deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan, it was time for me to move on and try my hand in the civilian world.

Fourteen years later and after various jobs in law enforcement and militrary contracting I am finishing my senior year at The University of Montana and hope to be finished at the end of the fall 2024 semester or no later than spring 2025.
